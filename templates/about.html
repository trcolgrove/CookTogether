About

CookTogether is a website used in order to allow friends, families, and colleagues to generate meals together with the ingredients they might have.

Just input your ingredients, invite friends and you'll be ready to go! CookTogether founders are Sylvie Abookire, Aditi Ashok, Thomas Colgrove, and Zoe Monosson.
